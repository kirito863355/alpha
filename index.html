About SAO

The perception of video games has fundamentally changed with the introduction of Full Immersion technology, which raises the level of perception of what is happening in the game to the maximum and completely cuts off the connection with the real world.
This technology is used to create a revolutionary in the VRMMORPG genre game called “Masters of Swords Online”, which meets all the standards of multiplayer role-playing games with the only difference that the player feels his character.
